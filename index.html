Get to know us

Research

At QUDYMA, we specialise in the exploration of the response of quantum materials, with a focus on low-dimensional systems, when pushed aways from equilibrium.
Our projects often start by developing a solid understanding of equilibrium structure, described with a variety of tools that include ab-initio approaches and effective models. We can then perturb the system under study using the large toolbox of numerical and analytic techniques. Notable fields of study of interest to us include attosecond physics in 2D materials, out-of-equilibrium superconductivity, topological materials and computational dynamics

Software

At QUDYMA, we study quantum dynamics far from equilibrium in realistic materials and devices. This demands advanced numerical methods combining ab-initio codes with in-house simulation libraries built for modern high-performance computing. Our main software packages include ATATA, EDUS, and Quantica.jl, alongside maintained legacy codes such as MathQ, IWERIA, and TimeProp.
